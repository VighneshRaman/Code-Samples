This is the content of Item 1.

  • Favorite Cartoons

  • Avatar: The Last Airbender

  • Dragon Ball Z

  • Superman: The Animated Series

  • Gundam Wing

Paragraph Header

The list to the right shows four of my favorite cartoon series. They are a mix of japanese anime and western animation. All of them feature epic stories and powerful heroes. Dragon Ball Z has by far the most powerful characters. but Avatar: The Last Airbender does the best job building its world. However, in the end, Superman: The Animated Series would end up leading into an established animated universe. Even though Avatar had a sequel, it never got as big as the DCAU. As for Gundam Wing, it is clearly the most political of the listed series. Though one of my favorites, it is the least enjoyable show on this list. The heavy-handed politics makes it almost boring at times.
